Bitcoin in Colombia

Nowadays the popularity of bitcoin has decreased, principally since the bitcoin price crash in December 2017, where in a single day a bitcoin went from worth USD $ 25,000 to USD $ 12,500. However, it is still the most popular cryptocurrency and one of the most used in the world, for example, here in Colombia every day we make transactions for more than USD $ 500,000 in bitcoins. (Data from the end of October 2019)


In Colombia there is a virtual platform that allows transactions with bitcoins. The platform LocalBitcoins.com is designed to allow the easy purchase and sale of bitcoins since COP $ 1.000.000 (one million Colombian pesos). The page is designed to allow users to carry out purchase and sale transactions through bank transactions, Efecty, Baloto or Western Union.


The objective of the website is to provide an easy, safe and fast way to buy / sell bitcoins with Colombian pesos.
