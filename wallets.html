How to make transactions?

Anyone with a bitcoin address can send and receive bitcoins from other users who also have a bitcoin address.

Your address is public, unique and identifies you with other bitcoin users. It is possible to access a bitcoin network through virtual wallets:

There are many options to choose:

  • Mobile wallets
  • Web wallets
  • Desktop wallets
  • Hardware wallets

Despite the multiple wallets, due to the practicality of carrying the wallet with you at all times, the most widely used wallet is the mobile wallet.
