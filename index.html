BITCOIN

Bitcoin is a new form of money. Everyone knows about money, we use cash or credit card to buy things, either in shop or online. This is the money we use every day, we can use it physically, like when we buy the breakfast; or we can use it electronically, like when we buy a product from another country.

Bitcoin works is a similar way but it is a completely digital currency, as bitcoins are created and stored ONLY electronically. They are used as a medium of online exchange.

The first cryptocurrency that started operating was bitcoin, since then many other cryptocurrencies have appeared with different characteristics and protocols such as: Ethereum, Litecoin, Ripple, etc.

Creation

Bitcoin, the first cryptocurrency in the world, was created in 2008 by Satoshi Nakamoto. It is unknown if it was a single person or a group of people who designed the bitcoin protocol under the pseudonym.

Bitcoin is the first decentralized digital currency, designed to send money through the Internet, from person to person without intermediaries in a very easy and secure way.

The word decentralized is the most important topic about bitcoin and it’s the mainly reason why its so relevant!


Main Properties
  1. Decentralized: It means bitcoin works without an administrator or a regulatory entity, for this reason bitcoin cannot be affected by inflation, deflation and other economic problems. This reason makes Bitcoin popular in countries with macroeconomic problems.

  2. Privacy: Regarding privacy, when you make payments with bitcoin you can do it anonymously. With the regular currency the whole financial system knows who makes each movement.

  3. Autonomy: When you make payments through banks or other institutions, you have to pay a percentage of your money, and if it is a payment from one country to another, you have to pay a large amount of your money. With bitcoin you can send and receive money instantly anywhere in the world at anytime at no cost.

  4. Security: Bitcoin works through a global network of computers, which mantain a public record of transactions that are distributed throughout the network. The transactions are recorded unanimously according to a complex mathematical principle, for this reason it is impossible to fake transactions or bitcoins.

Bitcoin price

The price of bitcoin only depends on the law of supply and demand, for this reason the price can’t be controlled by anyone, it only depends on the number of people who buy and sell bitcoins.

Whoever the bitcoin price can be immediately affected, for example, by a bad press report, by financial speculation or media speculation, or governments speculation, or by a simple comment from an important person.
